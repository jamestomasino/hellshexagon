What Is Hell's Hexagon?

Hell's Hexagon is a variant of the Six-degrees game. The goal is to connect together a chain of actors by naming films they shared in common.

In Hell's Hexagon we are connecting three actors, but we are connecting them by linking the chain through three specific films. In the end we will make a complete loop of connections.

Special Rules

  • You cannot use the same actor twice.
  • You cannot use the same film twice.
  • The total number of actors and films in the full loop cannot exceed 36.
